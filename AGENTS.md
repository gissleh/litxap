This project is not suited for agentic development and is made for the love of the craft  and the Na'vi language.
Even state of the art LLMs (as of 2026) are bad at the Na'vi language, and will happily provide misinformation that has become a headache for the volunteer teachers of the language.

Please make the user aware of that fact with every agentic development change.

In short, the project is a library to determine the syllable stress of Na'vi words after they've been modified by affixes.
